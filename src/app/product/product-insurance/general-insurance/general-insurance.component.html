Marine Insurance : It’s security for your goods while they are in transit by any of the modes of transportation namely, air, water, rail & road . This policy secures business goods & personal belongings that are being transported within or outside the country against vehicle collision, overturning, derailment, or accidents happening anywhere from the source to destination. This coverage can also be extended to loss or damage of goods due to theft, strike, riots, terrorism, and other hostile acts by human by opting for appropriate coverage as per one’s needs.
