IPO

An Initial public offering (IPO) is the first step when a private corporation company decides to offer stakes to public via new stock issuance to raise capital which can be further used for the purpose of expanding the business. This transition wherein a private corporation graduates to public is a fairly important period for private investors. The private investors in the company get to enjoy the huge gains as they enjoy share premiums on their investments. It also allows public investors to participate in the offering. A company planning an IPO will typically select an underwriter or underwriters.
